## Goal

Prevent unwanted auto-linking of number-like strings.

## Steps

1) Create a page with a few number-like strings (e.g. reference numbers, phone-like sequences).
2) View on mobile.
3) Apply a fix (HTML/CSS/content formatting) and re-test.
