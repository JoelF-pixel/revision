## What is this skill?

Setting basic page titles involves configuring the text that appears in browser tabs and is read by screen readers. This is essential for accessibility and helps users understand where they are.

## Why is it important?

- **Accessibility** – Screen readers announce page titles to help users navigate
- **User orientation** – Helps users know which tab contains your prototype
- **SEO basics** – Good practice that carries over to production development
- **Professional quality** – Proper titles show attention to detail
