## What is this skill?

Design system contribution involves refining prototype patterns to meet the quality standards required for inclusion in the official GOV.UK or NHS design systems.

## Why is it important?

- **Community benefit** – Share your work to help others
- **Quality improvement** – Contribution process improves your patterns
- **Recognition** – Contribute to widely-used government resources
- **Learning** – Understand how design systems are built and maintained
