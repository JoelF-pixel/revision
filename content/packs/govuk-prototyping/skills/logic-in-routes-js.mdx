## What is this skill?

Logic in routes.js involves moving complex decision-making out of templates and into server-side JavaScript, making code cleaner and enabling more sophisticated processing.

## Why is it important?

- **Cleaner templates** – Keep views focused on presentation
- **Complex logic** – JavaScript offers more powerful programming features
- **Reusability** – Share logic across multiple routes
- **Debugging** – Easier to test and debug than template logic
