## You can…

- use: **magnification = image size ÷ real size**
- rearrange the equation
- convert units correctly (mm ↔ µm)

## Common conversions

- 1 mm = 1000 µm
- 1 µm = 0.001 mm

## Common mistakes

- mixing units (always convert so both sizes use the same unit)
