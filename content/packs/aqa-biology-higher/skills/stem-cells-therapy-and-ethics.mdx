## You can…

- describe uses of **embryonic** and **adult** stem cells
- evaluate **risks** and **ethical issues**

## Useful compare points

- **Embryonic stem cells**: can become many cell types (pluripotent), but ethical concerns.
- **Adult stem cells**: more limited, fewer ethical issues.

## Risks

- infection
- rejection
- tumours (uncontrolled cell division)
