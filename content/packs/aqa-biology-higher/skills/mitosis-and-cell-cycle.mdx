## You can…

- describe the **cell cycle** (growth, DNA replication, mitosis)
- explain what **mitosis** produces and why it matters

## High-yield points

- Mitosis produces **two genetically identical cells**.
- Used for **growth** and **repair**.
- Before mitosis, DNA is **replicated** so each new cell gets a full set.
