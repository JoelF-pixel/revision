## You can…

- define a **stem cell**
- describe at least two **uses** of stem cells
- describe a basic **risk/ethical issue**

## High-yield points

- Stem cells can **differentiate** into different types of cell.
- Uses: treating diseases (e.g. diabetes), replacing damaged cells, growing tissues.
- Risks: infection, rejection, tumour formation.
- Ethics: embryonic stem cells involve destroying embryos.
