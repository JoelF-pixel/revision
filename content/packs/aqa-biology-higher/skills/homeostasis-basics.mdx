## What you need to know

- **Homeostasis** means maintaining a **stable internal environment**.
- Examples of internal conditions:
  - body temperature
  - blood glucose concentration
  - water content
- Control systems use:
  - **receptors** (detect change)
  - **coordination centres** (process info)
  - **effectors** (bring about a response)
